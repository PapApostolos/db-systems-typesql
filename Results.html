Notice 1


For TypeSQL with available content and for units=180 we have dev and test accuracy 86.7% which is an improvement in comparison with the author's model (85.5% and 82.6% respectively). This change is due to the increase of the LSTMs units > (180 instead of 120).

Notice 2


From the above diagrams we observe than the Seq2SQL model has different
behavior from the expected (we achieved 45% accuracy while it is expected to
be around to 60% ). To determine if this outcome comes from the limited number
of epochs (ep==7) or from implementaion we plot the epoch/acc. the where-diagram.
